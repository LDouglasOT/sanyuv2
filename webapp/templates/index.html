Our Services

At Sanyu Hospital, we provide expert, compassionate care tailored to women and children at every stage of life.

Maternity Care

Comprehensive prenatal, delivery, and postnatal services with experienced midwives and obstetricians.

Pediatric Services

Specialized care for infants and children, including immunizations and developmental check-ups.

Family Planning

Supportive consultations and safe contraceptive solutions to empower your reproductive health decisions.

Gynecological Services

Comprehensive women's health care including screenings, consultations, and treatments.

Ultrasound & Imaging

Advanced diagnostic imaging for accurate monitoring and early detection of complications.

Emergency & Ambulance

24/7 emergency services with dedicated ambulances and critical care support for mothers and infants.
